Home alone?

Suddenly in home office or quarantine #coronavirus, but the daily routine should go on? More and more people are in their own four walls these days, trying to structure their everyday life. This Webapp wants to help you.

Every day you are matched for the day with 10 random people. The invitation will be sent by e-mail. Communication takes place via video call.

Video Calls

The video calls are made with Jitsi Meet. Jitsi is a free and open-source multiplatform videoconferencing applications for the web, iOS and Android. The only prerequisite for participation is Internet access and a PC with webcam, web browser Chrome or Firefox or a smartphone with the free Jitsi Meet mobile app.
